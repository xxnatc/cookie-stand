Logo

Color Logo

Width: 150px (Minimum) Width: 200px Width: 250px

Original logo for Pat's Salmon Cookies should only be scaled in 50px increment as per its width. The minimum width allowed is 150px.

The colored logo should always be displayed on a light background. If the logo must be placed against a dark background, use the monochrome logo in white (#FFFFFF).

Monochrome Logo

Width: 100px
(Minimum)		 Width: 150px Width: 200px

Like the color logo, the monochrome logo should only be scaled in 50px increment as per its width. The minimum width allowed is 100px.

Light background (#2B2922 or #DC7A23) Dark background (#DC7A23 or #FFFFFF)

Do not display the monochrome logo in any color besides Maire Brown (#2B2922), Tahiti Gold (#DC7A23), or White (#FFFFFF). Maire brown should be used only against a light background, and White only against a dark background. Tahiti Gold can be used with either.
